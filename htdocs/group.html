Why Your Online Reputation Makes a Difference

July 17,2012 Dex One Contributer

According to a 2012 survey by SteelHouse, an e-commerce company, 98 percent of shoppers said that online customer reviews have a major influence on their decision to purchase a product or service. The survey also found that 72 percent of customers claimed they always read reviews before making a purchase. Most respondents read up to 10 reviews about a product or service before making a decision. So it's more important than ever to monitor what's being said about your business online.
